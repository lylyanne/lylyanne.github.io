Personal Values

21 September 2014

In general, I try to live up to the following values:

Disagree 1 ----- 2 ----- 3 ----- 4 ----- 5 Agree

  • Accountability - 4
    One of my pet peeve is seeing a project goes well and everyone wants to take credit and when the project doesn't go so well anymore, everyone points finger on who's fault that is. I find it important to get the credit that is due to me as well as owning up my mistakes
  • Grit - 5
    I believe life is like a roller coaster. There will be a time where everything just goes really really well and there will be a time when you just can't catch a break. It is important not to give up when things haven't gone my way.
  • Health - 5
    I believe this should be everyone's number 1 value. Think about a time when you are really sick. You realize that you don't want money, you don't want accolades, you don't want better relationships.You just want to start feeling better!
  • Helping others - 4
    I believe in karma so I believe in helping others to the extend I am able to. I also know my abilities and limitations and I am not afraid to say 'no' when I am not able to help.
  • Money - 4
    Money is not everything but it is essential because it provides a sense of security. Leaky roof? Car trouble? Loved one needs surgery? Having money solves those problems and let me focus on what's important.
